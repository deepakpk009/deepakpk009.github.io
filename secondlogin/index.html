Second Login

Multi-Profile Browser for Android

Manage Multiple Accounts with Ease

Second Login is a powerful multi-profile browser designed for Android users who need to manage multiple accounts across various websites. Create and maintain separate profiles for each of your online identities, making it easy to switch between accounts without the hassle of logging in and out repeatedly.

Download on Google Play

Key Features

Multiple Profile Management

  • Create unlimited profiles for each website
  • Easily switch between profiles with a single tap

Homescreen Shortcuts

  • Add website profiles directly to your device's homescreen
  • Access your favorite profiles instantly without opening the main app

Profile Customization

  • Edit website URLs and titles at any time
  • Modify profile names to keep your accounts organized
