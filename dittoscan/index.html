Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ditto Scan? +

Ditto Scan is an Android document scanner app that lets you capture or import pages, edit them, organize them into projects, and export PDFs.

Can I scan paper documents with my phone camera? +

Yes. Ditto Scan supports camera-based document capture with flash control and an optional grid overlay.

Can I import images from the gallery? +

Yes. You can import existing document photos from the gallery or Android photo picker.

Can I crop, deskew, and rotate pages? +

Yes. Ditto Scan lets you manually crop page corners, deskew the document view, and rotate pages before saving.

Can I create a document with multiple pages? +

Yes. Ditto Scan supports multi-page document projects and PDF export.

Can I rearrange pages later? +

Yes. You can reorder pages with drag and drop, add new pages, replace a page in place, or remove pages you no longer need.

Can I share a single page as an image? +

Yes. A single page can be shared as a JPEG from the preview flow.

What PDF settings can I choose? +

You can choose A4, Letter, or Original image size, plus Low, Medium, or High export quality.

Does Ditto Scan store files locally? +

Yes. The app stores projects and related files in app-private local storage on your device.

Does Ditto Scan work on tablets too? +

Yes. The app supports responsive layouts for phones and tablets, including flexible tablet-friendly screens.
