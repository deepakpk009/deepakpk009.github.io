Frequently Asked Questions

What is Simple USB Signage? +

Simple USB Signage is an Android TV app that plays photos, videos, and optional background audio from a connected USB drive as continuous digital signage.

Do I need an internet connection to use the signage playback? +

No. The main signage workflow is USB-based, so your photos, videos, and audio files can play locally from the connected USB drive.

Does the app scan folders and subfolders? +

Yes. The app recursively scans folders and subfolders on connected USB drives and builds a flat media list for playback.

What media can I use for signage? +

The app supports common image, video, and audio formats. Images and videos are used for the visual signage playlist, while audio files can be used as optional background music.

Can photos and videos play in the same signage loop? +

Yes. The app supports mixed image and video playback in the same continuous signage session.

Can I control how images fit on the TV screen? +

Yes. You can choose Fill screen for edge-to-edge display or Show full image to avoid cropping when the image aspect ratio does not match the TV.

Can I change the image slide duration? +

Yes. The image slide duration can be increased or decreased from the settings screen.

How does background music work? +

Audio files are played as a separate looping background playlist. You can also decide whether videos should play their own audio or stay muted while background audio continues.

What happens in demo/free mode? +

Free/demo mode limits the number of unlocked audio, video, and image files shown in the media set. Locked items are blurred and show a lock badge, and demo playback shows a subtle watermark.

Can I delete files from the app? +

Yes. Long-press OK on a media item to open the details dialog, where supported actions such as open and delete are available.
