About the app

BrowsePad

BrowsePad is built to redefine how people browse the internet on Android TV by turning a phone into a natural trackpad-style controller through BrowsePad Connect.

Redefining TV browsing

BrowsePad is not just positioned as another Android TV browser. It is designed around a different browsing model, where the TV screen becomes the browser canvas and the phone becomes the most natural way to point, scroll, zoom, and enter text.

  • Phone-as-trackpad control designed as a core browsing experience
  • BrowsePad Connect with QR-based pairing over the local network
  • Remote scrolling, zooming, clicking, and text input from the phone
  • TV-first browser interface that still supports tabs and desktop-style browsing
  • Works with a normal TV remote, but stands out when paired with a phone

What makes it different

BrowsePad Connect is the centerpiece of the experience. Instead of forcing browsing to feel limited by a D-pad alone, BrowsePad gives users a more fluid and direct way to interact with the web on TV.

The goal is not only to offer web access on Android TV, but to make TV browsing feel more usable, more precise, and more modern.
