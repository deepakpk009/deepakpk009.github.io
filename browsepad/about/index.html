About the app

BrowsePad

A TV-first web browser for Android TV with tabs, desktop-style browsing, an app-wide pointer, and BrowsePad Connect for phone-based remote control.

What BrowsePad is built for

BrowsePad is designed for large-screen browsing with a normal TV remote, while also giving users the option to pair a phone as a trackpad-style controller.

  • Multi-tab browsing with tab titles and favicons
  • Address bar support for URLs, domains, and search queries
  • Bookmarks, history, downloads, and session restore
  • Adaptive and desktop view modes for different websites
  • BrowsePad Connect with QR-based phone pairing
