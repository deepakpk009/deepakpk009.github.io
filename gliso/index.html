Frequently Asked Questions

What is Gliso Gallery Slideshow? +

Gliso Gallery Slideshow is a folder-based slideshow app for Android, designed especially for tablets. It turns local folders into reusable fullscreen slideshows for photos, videos, and optional background music.

Does Gliso work with local folders on my device? +

Yes. You choose folders through Android's document picker, and Gliso saves permission so those folders can be reopened later without repeating setup every time.

Can I save more than one slideshow setup? +

Yes. Each selected folder is saved as its own slideshow profile with its own settings, so different albums can behave differently.

Can photos and videos play in the same slideshow? +

Yes. Gliso supports mixed media playback, so photos and videos can appear together in one continuous fullscreen slideshow.

Can I play music during the slideshow? +

Yes. If the selected folder includes supported audio files, Gliso can use them as optional background music during playback.

Can videos play with their own sound? +

Yes. You can choose whether videos use their own audio or stay muted while background music continues.

What settings can I change for each folder? +

Each saved folder can keep its own photo duration, sort mode, subfolder preference, video audio behavior, and fit or fill screen mode.

Can Gliso include subfolders while scanning media? +

Yes. When you add a folder, you can decide whether Gliso should scan only that folder or include its subfolders too.

Is the app good for tablets? +

Yes. The home screen layout adapts to device size, using roomier grid layouts on tablets for easier browsing and repeat use.

Do I need to upload my media anywhere? +

No. Gliso is built around local folders you explicitly choose, so your slideshow media can stay on your device.
