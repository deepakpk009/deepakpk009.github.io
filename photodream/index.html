Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload photos to Photo Dream Screensaver? +

You can upload photos using Web upload with a secure token, Wi-Fi upload when on the same network, or FTP upload. QR codes make connecting quick and easy.

Do I need to be on the same Wi-Fi network? +

Not for web upload. The token-based web upload works even if your phone or PC is on a different network.

Can I show a clock on the screensaver? +

Yes. You can fully customize the clock including position, size, format, seconds, shadow, and burn-in protection.

How do I activate it as my screensaver? +

Use the Activate option inside the app to jump directly to Android TV’s screensaver settings.
